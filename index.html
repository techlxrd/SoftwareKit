AI usage in development

As a solo developer, I must admit that certain features and improvements were made with the help of AI because I do not have that knowledge yet. I also want to be honest about these things because some people have said that my app is completely vibe-coded. I do not like vibe-coding,actually I am against it because I believe it ruins creativity, but AI can sometimes be a useful tool for coding.

•AltStore repo support: I started working on AltStore repo support based on a friend's idea and improved and fixed it with the help of AI.

•The major performance optimizations introduced in v3.2 were made with the help of DeepSeek.

•The iPA Signer was mostly made with AI.

•The landing page was made with AI and improved by me because I suck at creating landing pages and don't have any ideas.

•The Liquid Glass effect from the HarmonyOS version was taken from a CodePen and improved with AI.

•The newsfeed from the HarmonyOS version was fixed with the help of AI because I did not know how to make it work due to CORS restrictions.

•The Device Information feature was improved with AI.

•The app library features were improved with AI.

•Some descriptions and text were written by me and improved with AI.

•Some CSS optimizations were made by AI

Btw, I still had to fix many things myself because AI created the feature, but it was broken and I had to change some things in order to make it work and it took lots of hours. The rest was built by me.
